These stories are intended as excellent examples of kitsoki stories. Never
paper-over, hack or workaround issues in the runtime - these stories exist to
expose those issues through real use cases and force their resolution.

Story edits must satisfy the user-messaging standard in
`../docs/stories/story-style.md` §3.4: from the first visible turn, free-form
captures, routers, imports, workbenches and long-running dispatches should show
what was understood, which path was selected, the mode/consequence, and the next
action. Interrupt only for required input, a real choice, or side-effect
approval; prove important messaging with no-LLM flow assertions.
